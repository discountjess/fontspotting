A flash card game
to aid with typeface recognition

Following our education and into our careers, we couldn’t simply look at typefaces and put names to all of them. So we made a flash card game for ourselves and thought it was
something worth sharing.

Dustin de Souza & Jessica Witt

That's them

Well, you could download the onscreen version below. Or you could grab the print ready version and get it done on 350gsm artboard with round corners like we did to be able to properly test yourself.

We don’t want you to part with your hard earned cheese. We would however appreciate your help in spreading the existence of our little project by ‘paying with a tweet’ to download the e-book.

If you are however feeling uncharitable or simply in a rush, feel free to download without 'paying', we’re happy to share.
